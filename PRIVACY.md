# Privacy

YTBar collects no data and has no servers. It runs entirely on your Mac.

- It reads the playback state of your YouTube tab locally, through AppleScript, directly from Google Chrome.
- Its only network calls fetch preview images (thumbnails / storyboard frames) from YouTube's public image servers, the same ones your browser already uses. Nothing about you is sent anywhere else, and there is no analytics or telemetry.
